About Me

Welcome to my Portfolio Website! I'm a San Diego based artist specializing in character design, concept art, and digital and traditional art. For the past decade I've used traditional art as a way to not only vent my emotions, but use as a hobby to grow and build upon. As I grew out of adolescence, I needed to find ways to continue my passions as a sustainable career. That's when I discovered the world of digital art. This portfolio showcases a small glimpse into my skills and digital art portraits.

My Work

My art started as a hobby during my freshman year of high school, when I was going through puberty and depression. Soon I realized how fun and satisfying the process was. It was a way for me to not only vent my darker emotions, but it also made me come to a great appreciation for the process, culture, and community of art. While i'm still growing as an artist, I hope you enjoy the works I've done so far!
